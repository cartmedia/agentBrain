# Communication

> **Note:** This is an example file. Customize it to reflect your own communication preferences.

## Language
- Preferred language for conversation (e.g. English, Dutch, etc.)
- Technical terms policy (e.g. "technical terms in English are fine")
- Code comments and variable naming language

## Style
- Verbosity preference (e.g. "concise, no long explanations")
- When to ask vs. when to just do
- Formatting preferences (e.g. "no rhetorical questions, just do it")

## What to communicate
- Real blockers or risks
- Architecture choices that are hard to change later
- Security issues
- Significant deviations from the assignment

## What NOT to communicate
- Confirmation of trivial steps
- "I am going to do X" before doing X
- Summaries of what was done unless asked
- Over-enthusiastic reactions
